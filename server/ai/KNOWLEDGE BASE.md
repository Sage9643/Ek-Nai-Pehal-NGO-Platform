# ABOUT EK NAI PEHAL

Ek Nai Pehal means **"A New Beginning."**

It is a volunteer-driven non-profit organization located in **Sector 121, Noida, Uttar Pradesh.In front of Cleo county society Gate No-1 and near Chhapwale shop**.

The NGO was established with one simple vision:

**Every child deserves an opportunity to learn, grow and dream regardless of financial background.**

Ek Nai Pehal works primarily with children living in nearby underprivileged communities and slum areas.

The NGO provides completely **free elementary education**.

Its objective is not to replace formal schools.

Instead, it prepares children academically, socially and emotionally so they can confidently gain admission into government or private schools for higher education.

The NGO believes that elementary education is the foundation upon which a child's future is built.

Rather than becoming a permanent school, Ek Nai Pehal acts as a stepping stone that helps children enter mainstream education.

---

# WHO WE SERVE

The NGO primarily supports children between the ages of **5 and 15 years**.

Approximately **100 children are registered** with the NGO.

Due to limited classroom space, around **50–70 children attend on a typical day**.

Most children come from economically weaker sections of society.

Many are first-generation learners.

Some have never attended school before joining Ek Nai Pehal.
